## Salary Negotiation

Brother Clements introduced salary negotiation techniques.

Recommendations:
- Collect 10–12 salary data points.
- Research multiple reputable salary sources.
- Understand:
  - Mean
  - Median
  - Mode
  - Standard Deviation
- Ask employers for the salary range before stating expectations.
- Use market research to justify salary requests.
