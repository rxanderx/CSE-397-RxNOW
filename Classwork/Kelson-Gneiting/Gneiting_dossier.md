## Principles Followed

- **Time boxing**: Use structured blocks for coursework, coding, and rest.
- **Pomodoro technique**: Alternate 25-minute focus sessions with short breaks.
- **Eisenhower matrix**: Prioritize urgent and important tasks first.
- **3-3-3 Rule**:
  - 3 hours -> deep technical work
  - 3 hours -> project and team collaboration
  - 3 hours -> review and documentation
