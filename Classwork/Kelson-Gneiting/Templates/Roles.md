# Roles on the Project

Besides being a developer on the project, you will be expected to fulfill specific roles on the project as we move through each iteration and delivery, you will be the subject matter expert. You will present topics, investigate and resolve issues, be assigned actions and interact with the customer. 

Each role must be fulfilled by a person. Some teams might need a person to fulfill more than one role. If the team has more member than roles, then  assign assistant roles, individuals who can take over when the principle role member is not around.

These responsiblities are on top of being a developer on the project. You will be expected to share in the responsiblites of deveolping the software as your primary tasking, and your secondary as the following role(s). 


* **Product Owner** (Interaction with the Customer)
    - Our customers are busy and we don't want the entire project sending communication to the customer. 
    - ***Responsiblities***: 
      - As the product owner, you are the main point of contact (POC) to the customer. All questions, meetings setups, and anything having to do with the customer is through you. 
    - ***Deliverables***: 
      - As the product ower you will help the team work through the Elicitation portion of the software development process. 
      - As the product owner you will document and Decisions that have been made.
    - ***Methodoloy***: As the product owner you will be resposible for planning and tasking the User Instruction manual

* **Scrum Master** (Planning and Tasking)
    - There are alot of working part in software development. Keeping each team member busy while delivery software is essential. 
    - ***Responsiblities***: 
       - The scum master will be encharge of creating tasking, meeting with the team to determine what tasks will be completed for the sprint/iteration. You will be responsible for closing of tasking, and velocity (story point collection).
       - As the scrum master, you will be responsible of the gitHub Issues, branches, and milestones, opening and closing branches.
    - ***Deliverables***: 
       - As the product ower you will help the team work through the Requirements and Design portion of the software development process. 
    - ***Methodologies***: As the product ower you will be responsible for Releases


* **Tech Lead (Front-End)**
    - We are building a full-stack operation and Front-End is the view and face of the product. 
    - ****Responsiblities***: 
        - As the Front-End Tech lead you will be responsible to make sure each feature is implemented. Your scope will cover the gui views to the customer. 
        - You will ensure that the system is following the MVC architecture with CRUD operations, by holding code peer reviews.
    - ***Deliverables***: 
        - As the lead you will be responsible for delivery of 1 feature for each person on the team every two weeks.
    - ***Methodologies***: 
        - As the lead, you will be responsible for the Developments and Building of the software. And the unit testing of each module.

* **Tech Lead (Back-End)**
    - We are building a full-stack operation and the Back-End is the control and heart of the product.
    - ***Responsiblities***
        - As the Back-End tech lead you will be responsible to make sure your team develops the interface requirements between the front-end and the backend. 
        - The Back end will be responsible for any external interfaces. 
        - You will ensure that the system is following the MVC architecture with CRUD operations, by holding code peer reviews.
    - ***Deliverables***: 
        - As the lead you will be responsible for delivery of 1 feature for each person on the team every two weeks.
    - ***Methodologies***: 
        - As the lead, you will be responsible for the Developments and Building of the software. And the unit testing of each module.

* **Tech Lead (Database)**
    - We are building a full-stack operation and the Database is the model and information of the system.
    - ***Responsiblities***
        - As the Database tech lead you will be responsible for the interface between the backend and the database. Your team will be responible for modeling the system's data and logging.
        - You will ensure that the system is following the MVC architecture with CRUD operations, by holding code peer reviews.
    - ***Deliverables***: 
        - As the lead you will be responsible for delivery of 1 feature for each person on the team every two weeks.
    - ***Methodologies***: 
        - As the lead, you will be responsible for the Developments and Building of the software. And the unit testing of each module, along with the Integration and Regession tests. 

* **QA Lead (Security, Testing)**
    - A QA lead enscures that the product is right and the customer got the right product.
    - ***Responsiblities***
        - The Quality Assurance lead will be responsible for reviews and secuity issues.
    - ***Deliverables***
        - Also will be responsible for planning and tasking the development of the Test Plan, including Acceptance and System tests. 
    - ***Methodologies***
        - As the QA Lead you will be responsible for the Operation and Monitoring of the delivered software.  

* **CM/DM (Configuration/Data Manager) - DevOps/CI**
    - The configuration/data manager works on making sure the product gets to the customer, the data is secure, and installation is seemless.
    - ***Responsiblities*** 
        - The Configuration/Data Manager you will be responsible for tasking the delivery of the product. This includes automation in the CD/CI of the product. You will be responsible for setting up the Operational Environment (server and database), installation scripts for user deployment. 
        - You will ensure that members of your team check in code in the correct branch and that the branches are merged. 
    - ***Deliverables***
        - Also will be responsible for planning and tasking the development of the Installation Guide.
    - ***Methodologies***
        - As the CM/DM, you will over see the Release and Deployment of the software.
