## Overview
Modern computing depends not only on writing software but also on connecting systems, exchanging data, and deploying applications. The following areas — networking architectures, tools, protocols, APIs, wireless systems, integration models, and hosting platforms — provide the backbone of how digital services are built and delivered today.
