# Overview

To better understand a software system, there are some major components that must be understood.
A typical large systems contains multiple tiers, intervaces, data and developmental languages.
This assignment will provide the background needed to develop these tiers and interfaces, by exploring the different technologies associated with each of these.

## Topics:
Please reseach, complare and contract, and provide uses for each of the following topics. One white paper per topic.

- **Tiers**:
  - **Front-End White Paper**: Research and compile a white paper comparing

  - **Back-End White Paper**: Research and compile a white paper comparing

  - **Database White Paper**: Reseach and complie a white paper comparing

  - **Interfaces**: Research the uses and applications of these interfaces: 
      * APIs,
