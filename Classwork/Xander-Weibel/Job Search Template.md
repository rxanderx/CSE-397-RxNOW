## Part 2: Reflection & Learning Journal

Answer the following for each job you applied to. Number your responses accordingly.
* What did you already know about this type of job or company before applying?
* What did you learn that was new or surprising when reading the job description?
* Were there qualifications or responsibilities that you didn’t expect?
* How did you tailor your resume or cover letter to fit this job?
* How did this application process affect your confidence or understanding of the job market?


## Part 3: Takeaways
